üìà Inflationary Gap

Introduced by: J.M. Keynes

  • Occurs when aggregate demand exceeds output at full employment (Yf).
  • AD = C + I + G + (X - M) > Yf ‚Üí leads to inflation.
  • Describes a disequilibrium where prices rise as long as gap persists.

  • Initial output: ‚Çπ600 crore | ‚Çπ100 crore taken as taxes ‚Üí ‚Çπ500 crore for spending.
